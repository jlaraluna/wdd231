OrbitTracker – Website Plan

Site Name

OrbitTracker – This name reflects the core function of the website: tracking satellites orbiting the Earth. It is concise, modern, and communicates a sense of space technology and motion.

Optional domain availability: orbittracker.space

Site Purpose

This website provides real-time and educational information about satellites orbiting Earth. Users can browse a curated list of satellites, explore key data like launch date and operator, and save their favorite satellites for later viewing. It serves as a learning tool and a tech showcase for space enthusiasts, students, and curious minds.

Scenarios

  • What satellites are currently orbiting above my country or region?
  • How can I learn more about the launch date and mission purpose of a specific satellite?

Color Schema

  • Dark Space (#0D0D0D): Used for headers, footer background, and overall brand tone.
  • Space Blue (#072359): Used for sections, menu and important containers.

Typography

  • Orbitron: Used for headers and site title to give a futuristic, tech-inspired look.
  • Roboto: Used for body text to ensure readability and a clean, modern appearance.
